Round 1: Good Fencers Make Good Neighbors

(16 points) Sharpen your wits for some cutting-edge trivia! Answer these two-part questions on swords for a point per part.



Question 1: Ask Russell Crowe: What fighters were named after the short sword used by the Roman legions? Ask Jon Snow: Since it didn't belong to any other "family" of blades, longswords used to be called WHAT swords?

Answer: Gladiators, Bastard






Question 2: In one version of the legend, King Arthur couldn't bleed to death while wearing the scabbard of what sword? William Wallace wielded what big-ass type of sword that lives on as the name of a remote-controlled mine?

Answer: Excalibur, Claymore






Question 3: The blades wielded by the Aztecs were made with what volcanic glass to keep them sharp? A sword made of meteorite was supposedly owned by what "Scourge of God" who died at his own wedding in 453 CE?

Answer: Obsidian, Attila the Hun






Question 4: For formal occasions, samurai carried two blades: The wakizashi and what longer type? The magical blade Kusanagi was given as an apology to the sun goddess Amaterasu, in what religion?

Answer: Katana, Shinto






Question 5: A bronze khopesh and two daggers were discovered when Howard Carter unearthed what teenager's tomb in 1922? In 1974, Colombian guerillas stole the sword of what South American liberator with a whole country named after him?

Answer: King Tut, Simón Bolívar






Question 6: Hugh Laurie's still waiting, but last year King Charles III used a sword to knight what former "QI" host? You can still see the sword Szczerbiec at Wawel Castle, where it was once used to crown kings of what European country?

Answer: Stephen Fry, Poland






Question 7: Giving us a nice metaphor for power, what mythical Greek kept a sword dangling over his head? Beowulf lost his sword Næġling, and eventually his life, to a fight with what mythical beast?

Answer: Damocles, Dragon






Question 8: In that "Alice in Wonderland" poem, a vorpal blade cut down what chortling monster? Really just an Elven dagger, what sword of Bilbo Baggins glowed when orcs were around?

Answer: Jabberwock, Sting






Question 9: Bonus: The Sentient World Observation and Response Department, or S.W.O.R.D., has appeared in the MCU as the successor to what defunct organization led by Nick Fury?

Answer: S.H.I.E.L.D.
